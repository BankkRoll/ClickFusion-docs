Welcome to ClickFusion, a library for React that adds dynamic and interactive effects to your buttons and links. Our library is designed to work seamlessly across a broad range of DOM elements including but not limited to Button, Anchor, Div, Paragraph, Span, and Heading. This ensures maximum flexibility when integrating ClickFusion into your projects.

## Steps to Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Installation">
    Install ClickFusion via npm to begin. [NPM](https://www.npmjs.com/package/clickfusion)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Basic Usage">
    Learn how to add simple effects to your DOM elements. [Quick Start Guide](/quickstart)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Advanced Customization">
    Dive into the advanced features and make the effects your own. [Custom Effects Guide](/essentials/customEffects)
  </Step>
</Steps>
